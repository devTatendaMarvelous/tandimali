About Us

A Registered "Credits Only" Microfinance

Tandi Imali Microfinance is a formally registered "Credits Only" institution whose mission is to bridge the financial gaps left by traditional banks — delivering tailored, accessible loans with a turnaround often as fast as 24 hours.

Our loans are primarily extended to Tapitana Smartpay payroll clients, with repayments handled seamlessly through salary deductions.

⚡

24-Hour Turnaround

Fast processing and disbursement — get funds when you need them most, without unnecessary delays.

🛡️

No Substantial Collateral Required

Flexible lending models that do not require traditional or substantial collateral to qualify.

📈

From Subsistence to Sustainable

We enable individuals to start or expand small businesses, transitioning them toward stable, sustainable income sources.

Our Services

What We Offer

Tailored financial products designed to empower individuals, civil servants, and small business owners across Zimbabwe.

💼

Salary-Based Loans

Short-term loans (1–6 months) for civil servants and employees, repaid conveniently through Tapitana Smartpay payroll deductions.

🚨

Emergency Loans

A financial safety net against unforeseen expenses — funds disbursed often within 24 hours so you are never left without support.

💻

Asset Financing

Finance personal equipment such as laptops and cellphones — enabling you to acquire the tools you need without straining your pocket.

🏪

Business Growth Loans

Working capital and income-generation loans that enable individuals to start or expand small businesses, moving from subsistence to sustainability.

🛡️

Flexible Collateral Models

Lending solutions that do not require traditional, substantial collateral — making credit accessible to more people across all income levels.

🎓

Educational Loans

Invest in your future with affordable educational loans — covering tuition, learning materials, and related academic expenses.

Get Your Loan in 3 Simple Steps

We have designed our process to be as simple and stress-free as possible.

1

Submit Your Application

2
