About Tandi Imali

Empowering individuals and communities through accessible, reliable, and swift financial solutions across Zimbabwe.

Our Story

Bridging the Gap for Everyday Zimbabweans

At Tandi Imali Microfinance, we recognized a significant gap in the financial sector: hard-working individuals and small businesses were often excluded from traditional banking channels due to stringent collateral requirements and lengthy approval processes.

We established our institution as a registered "Credits Only" Microfinance dedicated to providing tailored, fast, and accessible soft loans. By partnering with leading payroll systems like Tapitana Smartpay, we ensure our clients receive the support they need, exactly when they need it, fostering community development from the ground up.
