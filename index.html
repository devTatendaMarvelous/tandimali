Who We Are

A trusted partner for your financial growth

Tandi Imali Credits Only Microfinance

Soft Loans Built for Real People

Tandi Imali Microfinance is a registered "Credits Only" microfinance institution whose core aim is to foster the development of communities and individuals by granting them accessible, affordable soft loans.

Our loans are primarily extended to Tapitana Smartpay's payroll clients, making repayment seamless and straightforward — no complicated processes, no hidden charges.

Short Turnaround Period Quick application processing so you get funds when you need them most.
Attractive & Competitive Interest Rates Rates designed to be fair, sustainable, and within your means.
Professional & Competent Staff Our team is trained to serve you with integrity and expertise.
